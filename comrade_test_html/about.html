ABOUT

The Comrade Planner was made for our SE 319 Class: Construction of User Interfaces, at Iowa State University.
As a group, we all decided that it would be a great idea to create an interactive yet useful planner for users, specifically students in high school or college.
This was in result of other average online planners lacking the necessary customization to suit all users.
As students, we want to be able to customize our planners to suit all our needs in school and life in general.

Our goals for this planner is to:

- Create a website that allows users to make their own online customized planner
- Provide that customization necessary to fit all facets of our user's lives
- Import other calenders/applications such as Google Calender, Spotify, Slack etc
- Create a reminder/to-do list system for the user
